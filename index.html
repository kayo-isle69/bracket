Origin Story

Built from a Real Problem,
at a Real Cafe.

It started the way most good ideas do — with frustration. At our local gaming cafe, competitive matches were a weekly ritual. Different people showed up every session, lineups constantly shifted, and the energy was always high. So when a tournament broke out, we ran it the only way we knew how: paper brackets drawn on whatever surface was available.

It sounds simple. It never was.

We lost track of seedings. Someone always got accidentally skipped. The loser's bracket — when we even attempted one — turned into a mess of arrows, crossed-out names, and arguments about who was supposed to play whom. By the time we figured out the bracket, half the excitement was already gone.

We searched for apps and websites to help. What we found was a wall of friction: mandatory accounts, paywalled features, and ads interrupting every interaction. Most of them required an internet connection at all times — which doesn't work when you're in a local gaming session that's completely offline.

So the decision was made: build something better. Something built for the actual moment — fast to set up, easy for any host to run, and completely free to use. No sign-in. No subscriptions. No interruptions. Just the bracket, the players, and the game.

BRACKET is the result of that decision. Version one. Built to work offline, accessible from any browser, saved locally on your device. The tournament belongs to you — and it's ready the moment you need it.

"We needed more time for the actual tournament and less time figuring out the bracket. So I built the tool that should have already existed."
The Problems We Solved
📄
Paper brackets are unreliable Crossed-out names, arrows pointing everywhere, arguments about the correct match order — every paper bracket eventually became unreadable.
🔢
Loser bracket math is hard Nobody ever knew exactly how many matches the loser's bracket needed to reach a clean final. The count was always wrong.
🌐
Online tools required internet Playing a local LAN session? Most tournament websites broke completely without a connection. A local app shouldn't need the web to function.
🔒
Accounts and paywalls everywhere Every app wanted a sign-in for the host or a payment to unlock basic features. A bracket is a bracket — it shouldn't cost anything.
📢
Ads at every step Between every match. Before every round. Ads that reset the page, broke the bracket state, or simply got in the way of the moment.

How It Works

Two Brackets.
One Champion.

BRACKET uses double elimination — the fairest format for competitive play. Every player is guaranteed at least two matches before they're out, and the final champion has truly proven themselves against the whole field.

Winners Bracket
Stay Undefeated
Players who haven't lost compete here. Win your way through to the Grand Final and you enter as the favorites — with a built-in advantage.
  • 1Players are seeded and matched in Round 1
  • 2Winners advance to the next round
  • 3Losers drop to the Losers Bracket — they're not out yet
  • 4The last undefeated player reaches the Grand Final
Losers Bracket
Fight Your Way Back
One loss sends you here — but it's not over. Survive every match in the losers bracket and you earn your shot at the Grand Final.
  • 1Losers from the Winners Bracket enter each round
  • 2They face the survivors already fighting in the bracket
  • 3A second loss means elimination — no more chances
  • 4The final survivor reaches the Grand Final
